About Me

Hi, I am Surendra Mahara, passionate and driven individual with a background in B-Tech in Mechanical Engineering and experience as an Industrial Design Engineer and Drafter.

I am passionate about mechanical engineering, having completed my B.Tech in the field. My journey began with hands-on experiences during college, where I worked on various projects and gained practical insights. One highlight was my final year internship at a food machinery manufacturing company, where I focused on designing and optimizing machinery. This experience deepened my understanding of mechanical design principles and prepared me for the professional world.

Currently, I am employed as a Junior Design Engineer at an Australian-based company, where I am involved in diverse mechanical design projects. My role encompasses a wide range of tasks, including creating models, drafting for manufacturing, generating shop drawings, developing products, designing structures, conducting reverse engineering models, and working on piping systems. Each project challenges me to apply my technical knowledge and problem-solving skills effectively.

Working in this role has been an enriching experience. I've had the opportunity to collaborate with experienced engineers and learn from their expertise. I am continuously expanding my skills in modeling software, gaining proficiency in creating detailed designs that are ready for manufacturing. Drafting for manufacturing involves translating engineering drawings into instructions that production teams can use to build components accurately. It's a crucial step that ensures the designs meet quality standards and are feasible to produce.

Shop drawings are detailed plans that provide instructions for fabricating and assembling components. Developing new products requires creativity and a thorough understanding of market needs and technical requirements. It's about innovating solutions that are not only functional but also efficient and cost-effective. Structural design involves designing frameworks and support systems that can withstand loads and stresses, ensuring safety and durability.

Reverse engineering modeling involves analyzing existing products or components to understand their design and functionality. This process helps in improving or reproducing them with better materials or technologies. Piping design focuses on creating systems for fluid transportation within structures, ensuring efficient flow and minimal energy loss.

This proactive approach allows me to tackle new challenges with confidence and contribute effectively to my team's success.
